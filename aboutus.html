ABOUT THE LOGO

Name of School: DAVAO ORIENTAL REGIONAL SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL (Through Republic Act 9630)

Date of Approval: May 13, 2009

ERLENMEYER FLASK
Represents a power tower which is used for transmitting communications inviting other Schools Divisions of Region XI.
LIGHTNING
Produces magnet attracting other Schools Division of Region XI.
MOLECULES/ATOMS
Represent the different Schools Divisions of Region XI
PINK TRIANGLE
Its three corners represent the special subjects namely;MATHEMATICS,SCIENCE and ENGLISH.
YELLOW CIRCLES AND BLUE TRIANGLE
Represents Science and Mathematics concepts.
COMPUTER INSIDE THE PINK TRIANGLE
Represents technology which is integrated in all subjects areas.
COLOR GREEN
Symbolizes nature and the natural world.

OUR VISSION

Davao Oriental Regional Science High School is a center of excellence in science and mathematics education at
the secondary level that shall develop potential leaders in science and related fields.

OUR MISSION

To develop responsible and morally upright science oriented leaders through relevant and globally competitive
science and mathematics education programs. To make the school a center of excellence in science education for leaders and builders of the nation.
